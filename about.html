About Me

Hello! I’m Genevieve Awa, a passionate DevOps Engineer with a rich background in technology and a keen interest in AI/ML. My journey has taken me from Nigeria to Canada, where I’ve honed my skills and developed a deep understanding of cloud technologies and automation.

Educational Background

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Ibadan, Nigeria, where I developed a strong foundation in software development and systems design. Building on that, I pursued a Graduate Certificate in Artificial Intelligence with Machine Learning from Humber College, Toronto, to deepen my knowledge and expertise in cutting-edge technologies. I'm currently enrolled in a Graduate Certificate in Web Development from Humber College.

Professional Experience

With over 5 years of experience in the tech industry, I’ve worked on various projects involving cloud infrastructure, automation, and DevOps practices. My role as a DevOps Engineer has allowed me to design and implement scalable solutions, optimize CI/CD pipelines, and collaborate with teams to deliver high-quality software. I am skilled in using AWS and GCP, container orchestration, and infrastructure as code, which enables me to create efficient and secure environments.

Passions and Interests

Outside of my professional work, I have a deep love for fashion, music, and connecting with new people. I enjoy exploring new trends, sharing ideas, and staying up-to-date with the latest developments in technology and design. As an entrepreneur, I am always looking for opportunities to innovate and bring new ideas to life.

My Vision

I am committed to leveraging my skills and experiences to drive technological advancement and solve complex problems. My goal is to continue learning, growing, and contributing to projects that make a positive impact. Whether it’s through cloud solutions, AI, automation, or web/mobile development, I am dedicated to pushing the boundaries and achieving excellence.
